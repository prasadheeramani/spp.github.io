About Publications Group

We work on determining the structure of different materials and how those structural features influence physical and mechanical behaviour of those materials system.

Microstructural chateristics and crystallographic texture, formed due to thermal and mechanical processing of the materials and co relating this microscale result with the macroscale properties is the primary focus of our research.

Our choice of materials are usually light metal alloys and glass ceramics, but there is always a scope of expanding the horizon.For more details of our work go through the website or fell free to contact us.
